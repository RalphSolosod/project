Our BerTea Community

At BerTea, we believe in creating more than just a café – we're building a community. Our customers are the heart and soul of what we do, and we're proud to showcase our team and loyal patrons who make BerTea special.

Meet Our Team

Owner
The Owner’s love for coffee began during her travels across South America, where she discovered the art of specialty brews. Inspired by her journey, she founded BerTea to create a warm and welcoming space for the community—bringing exceptional coffee, teas, and heartfelt connections to everyone who walks through the door.
Mascot
Meet Dofu, our beloved mascot! While he may not have actual tea expertise, he brings something even better—pure joy and an unbeatable charm that brightens everyone's day. Whether greeting guests or sneaking treats, he's an essential part of our cozy café experience.
Barista
Emma is a true coffee artisan, dedicated to crafting the perfect cup for every guest. With a deep passion for lattes and brewing techniques, she ensures each drink is expertly made, balancing rich flavors and smooth textures. Whether it's a bold espresso or a creamy latte, Emma's skill and dedication make every sip unforgettable.
